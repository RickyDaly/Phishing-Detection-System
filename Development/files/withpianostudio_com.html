Learn, Play, and Perform

Welcome to With Piano Studio , your home for personalized piano instruction and collaborative music-making in the Park View neighborhood of Washington DC. Founded by internationally trained pianist and educator Victor Garcia , our studio welcomes students of all ages and skill levels—whether you're just beginning your musical journey or looking to refine your technique. In addition to private lessons , we offer collaborative piano services for vocalists and instrumentalists seeking an experienced partner for rehearsals or performances.

At With Piano Studio, music is more than a skill—it's a shared experience.

“I believe teachers should be a source of inspiration and guidance for their students, helping them develop a love for this art, by motivating and creating a pleasant and integrative learning experience for them, without, however, ignoring the demands that musical practice requires.”

— Victor Garcia, Founder of With Piano Studio
